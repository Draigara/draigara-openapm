# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or role.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- State assumptions and uncertainty.
- Give credit for prior work and upstream assets.
- Respect confidentiality and security disclosure processes.
- Avoid manipulative, discriminatory, or demeaning language.
- Accept maintainers' responsibility to protect project scope and safety.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, discriminatory conduct, deliberate disruption, doxxing, sexualised attention, publication of private information, supply-chain abuse, or knowingly misleading provenance/licensing claims are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. Maintainers may edit or reject contributions, issue warnings, temporarily or permanently restrict participation, or contact platform authorities where necessary.
